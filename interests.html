My hobbies

Hackathon

While working on my quiz game for Milestone Project 2 in JavaScript, Code Institute organized Hackathon for all students who were willing to participate. It was my first event of that kind where I could see how my future collaboration with colleagues would look like. Even on tight deadline we all enjoyed, and I will attend few more in future.

Video Games

Video games are often thought of as a source of entertainment that provides no good value, but they are like a workout session for the brain and can help in improving mental fitness. They can be educational and inspirational looking at great graphic design and effects - makes you admire the time and team effort invested in creation of ultimate gamers satisfaction. Video games often force us into thinking our way around an obstacle and help us to get better at critical thinking.

Cooking

Cooking requires skills such as organization, patience, diligence and good time management. It awakens many senses and can be a great release of endorphins.

Football

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world, so it is no surprise I was caught by its magic. Ever since I was kid, all my friends and myself were almost every day on concrete school pitch playing 5 or 6 aside, until eventually we started training together for the local club. This gave us amazing teamwork and also quite a lot of discipline. Even though now we all live in different parts of country and lead different kinds of life, we all still enjoy in this game, even if it's not professionally anymore.

Duolingo / Learning new languages

I developed interest for languages while travelling around European countries. Being familiar with local languages opens the door to better communication and stronger personal experience of country cultures.
